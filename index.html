Introduction

Hello and welcome to my page! I am Barbara. I am a former educator, who taught a mixed curriculum of computer science, astronomy, mathematics, statistics, and physics. I fell in love with web development, and decided to pursue it as a career path recently. I am very excited about the opportunities and challenges presented by web development in particular, and enjoy the various natures of the problems faced. I've taught a basic introduction to web development for years, and look forward to learning new technologies like React and Node.js while refining the skills I already have.

My dream job isn't a job so much as a series of facets. Why do you want to be a web developer?

Web development is something that has struck me for years as being a great career path. Between my love of problem-solving and my love of people, it just seems like a natural place to be. Web development is people-centric - offering detailed solutions to challenges people face on a daily basis - and demands high levels of solution-oriented thinking. I am here to learn how to be that solution-oriented thinker and incorporate the answers I know are achievable with those that are cutting-edge, and will help people live better lives.
